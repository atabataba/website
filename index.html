CHOP AND CARRY FARM

“To study the self is to forget the self. To forget the self is to be actualized by myriad things.” — Dōgen Zenji

Mission Statement

Chop and Carry Farm is an emerging Soto Zen practice (center) guided by the teachings of Dōgen Zenji and Suzuki Roshi, modeled on the early days of Tassajara. It is not a priest-training center.

The community remains small and within what the island can sustain. Life is built around ethical practice, manual work, and local self-reliance.

If this work interests you, please reach out at info@chopandcarry.com.
