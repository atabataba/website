CHOP AND CARRY FARM

“To study the self is to forget the self. To forget the self is to be actualized by myriad things.” — Dōgen Zenji

Mission Statement

Chop and Carry Farm is an emerging Soto Zen practice center guided by the teachings of Dōgen Zenji and Suzuki Roshi, with its practice structure modeled on the early days of Tassajara.

The community is intended to remain small and in harmony with what the island can support. Daily life is built around ethical practice and local self-reliance.

If this work interests you, please reach out at info@chopandcarry.com.
